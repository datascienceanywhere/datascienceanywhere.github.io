Founded in 2020, "**dataScience** Anywhere" was established with a mission to democratize education in data science and its real-world applications. 
Initially offering courses focused on machine learning, computer vision, 
and various data science techniques, the company quickly gained recognition
for its project-based approach to learning.
With nearly 14 courses developed, DataScience Anywhere has
empowered countless individuals to enter the field of data 
science with practical skills and knowledge.

Expanding its vision, "**datascience** Anywhere" now delves into fundamental 
concepts of mathematics and physics, bridging the gap between
theory and practice. By offering a more practical approach to 
understanding the world of science, the company aims to make 
complex subjects  accessible and engaging for students of all backgrounds.
With a commitment to excellence in education and a passion for
empowering learners, DataScience Anywhere continues to 
innovate and inspire in the ever-evolving field of data science and beyond.
